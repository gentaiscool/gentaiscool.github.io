I am a Ph.D. candidate at Electronic and Computer Engineering Department and Center for AI Research (CAiRE), The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology supervised by Prof. Pascale Fung. Previously, I completed my Bachelor in Computer Science at Institut Teknologi Bandung.

My current research interests lie primarily in the area of the Natural Language Understanding, Multilingual, Cross-lingual, Code-Switching, Dialogue, and Speech.

I serve as the Program Committee and Reviewer:
  • Conferences: ACL 2020, *SEM 2020, COLING 2020, EMNLP 2020, ICLR 2021, AAAI 2021, EACL 2021, NAACL 2021
  • Journals: TALLIP
  • Workshops: Humanizing AI at IJCAI 2019 and 4th Code-Switching Workshop at LREC 2020, SustaiNLP 2020
